LIVE IN HARMONY WITH NATURE, EMBRACED BY TREES

Welcome to Whispering Woods at Isle of Calm, a peaceful residential community in Powai, surrounded by the greenery of Aarey and Sanjay Gandhi National Park. This thoughtfully planned space brings together modern living and nature, giving you a quiet escape from the city's hustle. Spread across 3 acres, this gated community offers spacious 1 & 2BHK homes with private decks that provide stunning views of Vihar Lake and the lush surroundings. Enjoy sunlight-filled mornings and breathtaking skyline views at night, making your home a perfect retreat of comfort and luxury. Here, you’ll find the perfect balance between nature and modern conveniences. If you’re looking for fresh air, open spaces, and a peaceful environment while staying connected to the city, Isle of Calm is the ideal place to call home.

3 MILLION SQUARE FEET OF TRUST AND GROWING.

GHP Group, established in 1975, is a well-known name in real estate, education, and hospitality in Mumbai and Jaipur. With over 3 million square feet of developed space, GHP Group has created residential, commercial, and hospitality projects that mix city living with nature. The company is led by Chairman Mr. Prashant Sharma and Mr. Dikshant Sharma, and is recognized for its quality and dedication to building strong, lasting structures. GHP Group has also contributed significantly to education and hospitality, laying a solid foundation for future growth.
